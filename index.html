About IESL Robo Games 2018


The IT & Communication Engineering Sectional Committee of the Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka (IESL) in collaboration with Department of Computer Science and Engineering of University of Moratuwa proudly presents "IESL RoboGames 2018", challenging the youth of Sri Lanka to use their creativity to construct and program autonomous robots. RoboGames is a call to all the enthusiasts out there, it may be the pros eager to show off their colours, or the beginners waiting for a chance to enter this delightful world of automation.

Department of Computer Science and Engineering of University of Moratuwa is always willing to extend a helping hand, to answer your questions and to solve your doubts. It’s time to rack your brains and come up with an innovative design that deserves the title ‘Winner of RoboGames 2018’ The competition is expected to be held at the Annual Engineering Exhibition – Techno at BMICH. Awards will be presented at the conclusion of the Techno 2018 Exhibition.



School Challenge

The Information Technology and Communications Engineering Sectional Committee of the IESL organizes
one of its annual competitions; the “IESL Robotic Games ”, to promote Robots development among school students
and to recognize their talents as the next generation of professionals in the country.
So now it’s time to show your colours to the whole country as a young mobile application developer.









Undergraduate Challenge

If you are enthusiastic about Robotic, eager to learn more about Robotic development
and want to show your talents to the whole country by being a winner, then IESL Robo Games is for you.
Come up with an well Designed robot under the given rules, become a winner of “IESL Robo Games 2018”.
